Technology encompasses tools, systems, and methods created to solve problems and enhance human capabilities. Its evolution spans from rudimentary inventions like the wheel to advanced artificial intelligence and genetic engineering. Technology has revolutionized communication, transportation, healthcare, and more, shaping modern society.
Its impact is multifaceted, facilitating global connectivity, economic growth, and efficiency while also raising ethical concerns and disparities. From the Industrial Revolution to the digital age, technology continues to shape our lives, offering both opportunities and challenges as it transforms the way we work, interact, and perceive the world.
